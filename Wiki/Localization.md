# Localization
The App currently supports the below locales.

1. ar-SA
2. de-DE
3. en-US
4. es-ES
5. fr-FR
6. he-IL
7. it-IT
8. ja-JP
9. ko-KR
10. pt-BR
11. pt-PT *(alias of `pt-BR`)*
12. ru-RU
13. zh-CN
14. zh-TW

You can find the translations in `Source/CompanyCommunicator/ClientApp/public/locales` and the resource strings can be found in `Source/CompanyCommunicator.Common/Resources`.

**NOTE:** You can add/update the resources for other locales and update the configuration.
